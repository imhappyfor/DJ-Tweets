Welcome to Dj-tweets!

This site allows you to turn your favorite Twitter account into a composition of emotions! More specifically, it's a synthesizer for tweets. We first look up a Twitter account and pull that user's most recent tweets and process them with sentiment analysis. Using emotional classifications of keys set forth by Christian Friedrich Daniel Schubart in his treatise Ideas Towards An Aesthetic of Music, we build melodies based on the emotional content of the pulled tweets. Once we are done composing, we let MusicRNN take over and continue where we left off! To get started, simply type in a Twitter username and click search.

This site uses Tone.js, Paralleldots emotional sentiment analysis, MusicRNN, Twitter's API, p5.js & Tonal.js.
